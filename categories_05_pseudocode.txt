Recipient Selection & Cycle Management

FUNCTION Process_Recipient_Selection()
    CALCULATE: Total Donors in Current Cycle
    DETERMINE: Number of Intervals (Based on Donor Count / 1,386)
    RANDOMLY SELECT: Recipients for Each Interval
    ASSIGN: $1 Micro-Donations to Selected Recipients
END FUNCTION
